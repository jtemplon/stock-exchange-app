The Mid-Major Madness Stock Exchange

About

Welcome to The Mid-Major Madness Stock Exchange.

Basics

Teams: All teams outside of the six power conferences.

Starting Cash: $500

Who Made This?: The idea was originally conceived by Chris Schutte of Mid-Major Madness. The app was built by John Templon.

How Do Prices Work?: Prices are directly related to a team's adjusted efficiency margin on KenPom. (The pseudo formula is: KenPom AdjEM + abs(min(KenPom AdjEM)) + 0.10.)
